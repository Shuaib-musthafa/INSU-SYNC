How This Model Works

This simulation is based on industry research about Medicare Star Ratings:

  • Impact Factors: Different features affect Star Rating measures differently. For example:
    • Digital engagement strongly affects CAHPS measures (triple-weighted)
    • Care coordination significantly impacts chronic condition management
    • Provider tools improve accuracy of provider directories
  • Implementation Quality: Well-executed programs deliver better results
  • Market Factors: Competitive markets often see faster improvements
  • Financial Impact: Includes both CMS bonus payments and member retention benefits
  • Diminishing Returns: Higher penetration rates have less marginal impact
